SOFTWARE AS A SERVICE SUBSCRIPTION AGREEMENT

This SaaS Subscription Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of February 15, 2024, between CloudPlatform Corp., a Delaware corporation ("Provider"), and RetailCo Inc., a Texas corporation ("Customer").

SECTION 1. SUBSCRIPTION SERVICES
Provider grants Customer a non-exclusive, non-transferable right to access and use the Provider's cloud-based software platform ("Platform") during the Subscription Term solely for Customer's internal business purposes. Access is limited to the number of user seats specified in the applicable Order Form.

SECTION 2. FEES AND PAYMENT
Customer shall pay all subscription fees set forth in the Order Form on a monthly basis, due within fifteen (15) days of invoice. Provider may increase subscription fees upon sixty (60) days prior written notice to Customer. All fees are non-refundable except as expressly set forth herein.

SECTION 3. SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT
Provider shall use commercially reasonable efforts to make the Platform available with 99.5% uptime measured monthly, excluding scheduled maintenance. In the event of downtime below the SLA threshold, Customer shall receive service credits of 5% of monthly fees for each hour of excess downtime, up to a maximum of 15% of monthly fees.

SECTION 4. DATA OWNERSHIP AND SECURITY
Customer retains all rights to data it submits to the Platform ("Customer Data"). Provider shall implement industry-standard security measures to protect Customer Data. Provider shall not access Customer Data except as necessary to provide the services or as required by law.

SECTION 5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
Provider retains all rights to the Platform, including all underlying software, algorithms, and documentation. Customer grants Provider a limited license to use Customer Data solely to provide the services. Provider may use aggregated, anonymized data derived from Customer Data to improve the Platform.

SECTION 6. TERM AND TERMINATION
The initial Subscription Term is one (1) year, automatically renewing for successive one-year terms unless either party provides sixty (60) days written notice of non-renewal. Customer may terminate for cause upon thirty (30) days written notice if Provider materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure within thirty (30) days.

SECTION 7. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
Provider's total liability shall not exceed the fees paid by Customer in the three (3) months preceding the claim. Neither party shall be liable for indirect or consequential damages.
