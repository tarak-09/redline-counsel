EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of April 1, 2024, between Nexus Technologies Inc., a California corporation ("Company"), and Jordan Lee ("Employee").

SECTION 1. POSITION AND DUTIES
Company agrees to employ Employee as Senior Software Engineer. Employee shall report to the VP of Engineering and perform duties consistent with the position as reasonably assigned by the Company from time to time. Employee agrees to devote full business time and attention to the performance of Employee's duties. The Company reserves the right to change Employee's title, reporting structure, or responsibilities at any time.

SECTION 2. COMPENSATION
Company shall pay Employee a base salary of $165,000 per year, payable in accordance with the Company's standard payroll schedule. Employee shall be eligible for an annual performance bonus of up to 10% of base salary, based on the achievement of individual and company performance targets as determined by the Company in its sole discretion, with no guaranteed minimum payout. The Company reserves the right to modify the bonus plan structure at any time.

SECTION 3. BENEFITS
Employee shall be entitled to participate in the Company's standard employee benefit programs, including health insurance and 401(k) plan, subject to the terms of those plans and applicable law. Employee shall accrue ten (10) days of paid time off per year. Unused PTO shall not carry over and shall be forfeited at year-end.

SECTION 4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
Employee agrees that all inventions, discoveries, and works of authorship created by Employee at any time during or after employment that in any way relate to the Company's current or anticipated business, or that are made using Company resources, shall be the sole property of the Company. Employee waives all moral rights in such work product. This assignment extends to work created on Employee's personal time if it relates to the Company's field of business.

SECTION 5. NON-COMPETE AND NON-SOLICITATION
During employment and for twenty-four (24) months thereafter, Employee shall not: (a) engage in any business activity that directly or indirectly competes with the Company anywhere in the world; or (b) solicit, hire, or attempt to solicit any employee, contractor, or customer of the Company.

SECTION 6. AT-WILL EMPLOYMENT AND SEVERANCE
Employment is at-will and may be terminated by either party at any time for any reason. In the event of termination by the Company without cause, Employee shall receive two (2) weeks of base salary as severance, contingent on execution of a general release of all claims, including claims under ADEA, and compliance with the non-compete and non-solicitation obligations.

SECTION 7. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware, notwithstanding the Employee's place of work.
