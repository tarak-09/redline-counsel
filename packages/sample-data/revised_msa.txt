MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Master Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 1, 2024, between Acme Corp, a Delaware corporation ("Customer"), and TechVendor Inc., a California corporation ("Vendor").

SECTION 1. SERVICES
Vendor agrees to provide software development and consulting services as described in individual Statements of Work ("SOW") executed under this Agreement. Each SOW shall describe the specific services, deliverables, timelines, and fees applicable to that engagement.

SECTION 2. PAYMENT TERMS
Customer shall pay all undisputed invoices within sixty (60) days of receipt ("Net-60"). Invoices shall be submitted monthly in arrears. Late payments shall accrue interest at 0.5% per month on the outstanding balance.

SECTION 3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All pre-existing intellectual property of each party shall remain the property of that party. All deliverables, work product, inventions, and developments created by Vendor in connection with the services, including all intellectual property rights therein, shall be deemed "work made for hire" and shall be the sole and exclusive property of Customer. Vendor hereby assigns all rights, title, and interest in such work product to Customer. Vendor retains no license to use Customer's work product after termination.

SECTION 4. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
Each party's total cumulative liability arising out of or related to this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid or payable by Customer to Vendor in the three (3) months immediately preceding the event giving rise to the claim. Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, consequential, or punitive damages.

SECTION 5. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement or any SOW for convenience upon ninety (90) days prior written notice to the other party. Either party may terminate immediately upon written notice if the other party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within fifteen (15) days of receiving written notice of the breach.

SECTION 6. CONFIDENTIALITY
Each party agrees to maintain in strict confidence all Confidential Information disclosed by the other party and to use such information solely for purposes of performing its obligations under this Agreement. These obligations shall survive termination for a period of five (5) years.

SECTION 7. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Delaware, without regard to its conflict of laws provisions. Any disputes shall be resolved in the state or federal courts located in Wilmington, Delaware.

SECTION 8. ENTIRE AGREEMENT
This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to its subject matter and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, and agreements.
